# Breadfast Egypt - Product & Market Data

## Overview

**Breadfast** is Egypt's leading quick-commerce grocery delivery app with a vertically integrated supply chain.

- **Founded:** 2017 by Mostafa Amin, Muhammed Habib, Abdallah Nofal
- **Funding:** USD 30mn (valued at USD 250mn as of 2023)
- **SKUs:** 5,000-7,000+ products
- **Fulfillment Centers:** 39 locations in Cairo, Giza, Alexandria, Mansoura
- **Delivery:** Within 60 minutes, 24/7 availability
- **Delivery Fee:** EGP 15-20 per order
- **Average Order Value:** ~EGP 250

**Source:** [Breadfast Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Breadfast), [Deep Dive into Breadfast](https://vreader.substack.com/p/a-deep-dive-into-breadfast)

## Price Comparison: Breadfast vs Others

### Market Positioning
| Store | Positioning | Strength |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Breadfast** | Convenience + Quality | Fresh bakery, 60-min delivery, private label |
| Carrefour | Budget | Lowest prices, huge selection |
| Seoudi | Premium | Quality imports, fresh foods |
| Gourmet | Premium | Artisanal, organic, imported |
| Talabat Mart | Speed | Quick delivery, general items |

### Breadfast Competitive Advantages
1. **Fresh Bakery** - Baked daily, delivered early morning
2. **Speed** - 60-minute delivery
3. **24/7 Availability**
4. **Private Label Products** - Unique items not found elsewhere
5. **Vertical Integration** - Quality control

### When Breadfast May Be Cheaper
- Private label bakery items (no middleman)
- Byld protein bars (direct manufacturing)
- Fresh produce (direct sourcing)
- Bundle deals

### When Others May Be Cheaper
- Branded CPG products (Carrefour bulk pricing)
- Imported items (Gourmet specialization)
- Generic staples (Kazyon, local markets)

## Market Data

### Target Market
- Top 10% urban households in Greater Cairo & Alexandria
- Market size: EGP 41.5 billion (~USD 1 billion)
- Average household spend: EGP 59,000/year on food

### Egypt Online Grocery Market (2024)
- Total: USD 162-509 million
- Penetration: Only 0.5%-1% of total grocery sales
- Carrefour Egypt leads with ~USD 67mn online sales
