# Macronutrients Guide

## The Three Macros

| Macro | Calories/gram | Primary Function |
|-------|---------------|------------------|
| Protein | 4 | Muscle building, repair |
| Carbohydrates | 4 | Energy, performance |
| Fat | 9 | Hormones, absorption |

## Protein

### Daily Requirements
| Goal | Protein (g/kg bodyweight) |
|------|---------------------------|
| Sedentary | 0.8-1.0 |
| Fat Loss | 1.6-2.2 |
| Muscle Gain | 1.6-2.2 |
| Athletes | 1.8-2.5 |

|------|--------------|-------|

### Protein Timing
| Time | Amount | Purpose |
|------|--------|---------|
| Breakfast | 20-40g | Break fast, muscle synthesis |
| Post-workout | 25-40g | Recovery, growth |
| Before bed | 20-40g | Overnight recovery |
| Each meal | 25-40g | Even distribution optimal |

## Carbohydrates

### Types
| Type | Digestion | Best For |
|------|-----------|----------|
| Simple (sugar) | Fast | Post-workout, quick energy |
| Complex (starch) | Slow | Sustained energy |
| Fiber | Not digested | Digestion, fullness |

## Fats

### Types
| Fat Type | Effect | Sources |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Saturated | Limit | Meat, dairy, ghee |
| Monounsaturated | Healthy | Olive oil, avocado |
| Polyunsaturated | Essential | Fish, nuts, seeds |
| Trans | Avoid | Fried foods, margarine |

### Egyptian Fat Sources
| Food | Fat/serving | Type |
|------|-------------|------|
| Olive oil (1 tbsp) | 14g | Mono |
| Ghee (1 tbsp) | 13g | Saturated |
| Tahini (2 tbsp) | 16g | Poly/Mono |
| Nuts (30g) | 15g | Mixed |
| Eggs (1 large) | 5g | Mixed |
| Cheese (30g) | 9g | Saturated |

### Minimum Fat Intake
- Men: ~60-80g/day
- Women: ~45-65g/day
- Never go below 0.5g/kg bodyweight

## Quick Macro Math

### Protein Target (80kg person, muscle gain)
80kg × 2g = **160g protein/day**

### Converting to Calories
- 160g protein × 4 = 640 cal
- 300g carbs × 4 = 1200 cal
- 70g fat × 9 = 630 cal
- **Total: 2470 calories**
