# SECTION 7: SUPPLEMENT SAFETY & USAGE

## General Guidelines

### Dosing Times
| Supplement | When to Take |
|------------|--------------|
| Whey Protein | Post-workout, or anytime for protein goals |
| Casein | Before bed |
| Creatine | Any time, consistency matters |
| Pre-workout | 20-30 min before training |
| BCAAs | During or around training |
| Fish Oil | With meals |
| Multivitamin | With meals |
| Melatonin | 30 min before bed |

### Who Should Consult Doctor First
- Pregnant or breastfeeding women
- People with kidney disease
- People with liver disease
- People on blood thinners
- People with heart conditions
- Anyone under 18 (for performance supplements)
- Anyone on prescription medications

### Red Flags (Stop Taking & Seek Help)
- Rapid heartbeat
- Difficulty breathing
- Severe stomach pain
- Allergic reactions (hives, swelling)
- Jaundice (yellowing skin/eyes)
- Unusual bleeding
