# Egyptian Market Supplements & Fitness Products Database

## Overview

This database contains supplements, vitamins, and fitness products available in Egypt for trainers to recommend to clients. All prices are in Egyptian Pounds (EGP) and reflect 2024-2025 market prices (subject to change).

**Important Notes:**
- Always advise clients to consult a doctor before starting any supplement
- Prices fluctuate with USD exchange rate
- iHerb ships to Egypt but may have customs delays
- Local stores often have better prices for popular brands

## TRAINER RECOMMENDATION GUIDELINES

### For Beginners (First 6 months)
1. **Essential:** Multivitamin (Centrum or Opti-Men)
2. **Helpful:** Whey protein if diet lacking
3. **Optional:** Vitamin D (most Egyptians are deficient)

### For Intermediate (6 months - 2 years)
1. **Essential:** Whey protein, Creatine
2. **Helpful:** Multivitamin, Omega-3
3. **Optional:** Pre-workout (if needed for energy)

### For Advanced (2+ years)
1. **Essential:** Whey/Casein blend, Creatine
2. **Helpful:** Pre-workout, BCAAs/EAAs, Joint support
3. **Optional:** Beta-alanine, L-Carnitine

**Minimum Budget (Under 500 EGP/month):**
- Local multivitamin (Vitacid)

- Egyptian Vitamin D (Vidrop)

- ON Gold Standard or similar whey
- Good multivitamin
- Creatine

**Premium Budget (1000+ EGP/month):**
- Premium protein brand
- Complete supplement stack
- Specialty supplements as needed
