## 8.1 Cooking Methods & Calorie Impact

| Method | Calorie Change | Best For |
|--------|----------------|----------|
| Grilling | No added calories | Meat, chicken, fish |
| Boiling | No added calories | Vegetables, eggs, legumes |
| Steaming | No added calories | Vegetables, fish |
| Baking | +10-20 cal (if oiled) | Chicken, fish, vegetables |
| Sautéing (1 tbsp oil) | +120 calories | Vegetables, meat |
| Deep Frying | +100-200 calories | Avoid for diet |
| Air Frying | +10-20 calories | Alternative to deep fry |
