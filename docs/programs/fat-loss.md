# Fat Loss Training Program

## Overview
- **Frequency**: 4-5x per week
- **Duration**: 45-60 minutes
- **Goal**: Maximize fat loss while preserving muscle
- **Diet**: Caloric deficit required (TDEE - 500)

## Weekly Schedule
| Day | Workout | Duration |
|-----|---------|----------|
| Monday | Full Body Strength | 45 min |
| Tuesday | HIIT Cardio | 25 min |
| Wednesday | Full Body Strength | 45 min |
| Thursday | LISS Cardio | 30-45 min |
| Friday | Full Body Strength | 45 min |
| Saturday | Active Recovery | 30 min |
| Sunday | Rest |

## Full Body Strength Workout

| Exercise | Sets | Reps | Rest |
|----------|------|------|------|
| Barbell Squat | 4 | 8-10 | 90s |
| Bench Press | 3 | 8-10 | 90s |
| Barbell Row | 3 | 8-10 | 90s |
| Romanian Deadlift | 3 | 10-12 | 90s |
| Overhead Press | 3 | 10-12 | 60s |
| Lat Pulldown | 3 | 10-12 | 60s |
| Face Pulls | 2 | 15-20 | 45s |
| Plank | 3 | 30-45s | 45s |

**Note:** Keep weights challenging. Strength training preserves muscle in a deficit.

## HIIT Cardio Options

| Phase | Duration | Intensity |
|-------|----------|-----------|
| Warm-up | 3 min | Easy jog |
| Sprint | 30 sec | 90% effort |
| Rest | 60-90 sec | Walk |
| *Repeat* | 8-10 rounds | - |
| Cool-down | 3 min | Walk |

## LISS Cardio Options
- Brisk walking (incline treadmill)
- Cycling (moderate pace)
- Swimming
- Elliptical
- **Target:** 120-140 BPM for 30-45 min

## Active Recovery
- Light walking
- Stretching/yoga
- Foam rolling
- Swimming (easy)

## Fat Loss Nutrition Keys

### Daily Targets
| Macro | Amount | Notes |
|-------|--------|-------|
| Calories | TDEE - 500 | ~0.5 kg/week loss |
| Protein | 2-2.2g/kg | Preserve muscle |
| Fat | 0.8-1g/kg | Hormones |
| Carbs | Remainder | Fuel workouts |

### Egyptian Food Tips for Fat Loss
| Choose | Avoid/Limit |
|--------|-------------|
| Grilled meats | Fried foods |
| Ful (no oil) | Koshari (high cal) |
| Salads | Bread excess |
| Fish | Desserts |
| Lentil soup | Sugary drinks |

## Weekly Check-In
- Weigh yourself weekly (same day/time)
- Take progress photos monthly
- Adjust calories if plateau >2 weeks
- Prioritize sleep (7-9 hours)

## Common Mistakes
| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Too much cardio | Keep strength priority |
| Cutting calories too low | Minimum 1500 (men), 1200 (women) |
| Not enough protein | Track protein daily |
| Skipping weights | Strength preserves muscle |
| Impatience | Trust the process |
