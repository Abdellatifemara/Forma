# Upper/Lower Split (4-Day)

## Overview
- **Frequency**: 4x per week
- **Duration**: 60 minutes
- **Level**: Beginner-Intermediate
- **Goal**: Balanced strength and hypertrophy

## Weekly Schedule
| Day | Workout |
|-----|---------|
| Monday | Upper A |
| Tuesday | Lower A |
| Wednesday | Rest |
| Thursday | Upper B |
| Friday | Lower B |
| Saturday | Rest or Cardio |
| Sunday | Rest |

## Progression

### Week to Week
- Add 1 rep when possible
- When hitting top of rep range for 2 sessions:
  - Upper body: +2.5 kg
  - Lower body: +5 kg

### Deload (Every 4-6 weeks)
- Reduce weight by 40%
- Reduce sets by 1-2
- Focus on form

## Advantages
- Good recovery between sessions
- Trains each muscle 2x/week
- Balanced push/pull
- Flexible scheduling
- Good for most goals
