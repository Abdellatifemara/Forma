# FitApp Workout Programs Database

## Overview

Complete workout programs for all levels and goals. Each program includes exercise selection, sets, reps, rest times, and progression guidelines.

**Program Selection Guide:**
- **Beginner (0-6 months):** Full Body 3x/week
- **Intermediate (6-18 months):** Upper/Lower or PPL
- **Advanced (18+ months):** PPL or specialized splits

## Fat Loss Notes

3. **Progressive overload still matters** - Don't just do cardio
4. **Sleep 7-9 hours** - Recovery aids fat loss
5. **Stay hydrated** - 3+ liters daily
6. **Manage stress** - Cortisol affects fat storage

# PROGRAM 8: "RETURN AFTER BREAK" (2-4 weeks off)

## Week 1: Reintroduction (50% intensity)
- 3 days: Full body, light weights
- 2 sets per exercise
- Focus on movement quality
- No failure, stop 3-4 reps short

## Week 2: Building Back (70% intensity)
- 3-4 days
- 3 sets per exercise
- Moderate weights
- Stop 2 reps short of failure

## Week 3: Near Normal (85% intensity)
- Normal split
- Normal sets
- Working sets closer to failure
- May still have some DOMS

## Week 4: Full Return (100%)
- Return to normal program
- Normal intensity
- Full progression

## Key Rules for Returning
1. **Start lighter than you think** - Ego aside
2. **DOMS is normal** - Will be worse than usual
3. **Hydrate extra** - Body relearning
4. **Sleep more** - Recovery demands higher
5. **Don't compare** - You'll regain quickly
6. **Protein stays high** - Muscle memory needs it

---

## PROGRESSION GUIDELINES (All Programs)

### When to Increase Weight
- Hitting top of rep range for all sets
- Form remains excellent
- Rest periods don't need extending
- No excessive fatigue

### How Much to Increase
| Lift Type | Increase Amount |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Squat/Deadlift | 2.5-5 kg |
| Bench/Row | 1.25-2.5 kg |
| Isolation | 1-2.5 kg |
| Bodyweight | Add rep, add set, harder variation |

### Deload Guidance
- Every 4-6 weeks, or when needed
- Reduce volume 40-50%
- Keep intensity moderate
- Focus on technique
- 1 week duration
