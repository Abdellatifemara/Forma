## Fitness Tips
- **Fat loss**: Bran bread, brown rice, oats - higher fiber
- **Post-workout**: White rice/baladi - fast digesting carbs
- **Muscle gain**: Any - meet calorie needs
- **Volume eating**: Bulgur, freekeh (filling, lower calorie)

## Portion Guide
- 1 cup cooked rice = ~200 cal
- 1 baladi loaf = ~250 cal
- 1/2 cup dry oats = ~150 cal
