## Fitness Tips
- **Fat loss**: Molokhia (low cal), grilled meats
- **Post-workout**: Koshari, rice dishes
- **High calorie days only**: Fatta (very calorie dense)
- **Protein boost**: Add extra chicken/meat to any dish
