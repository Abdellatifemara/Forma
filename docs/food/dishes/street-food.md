## Fitness-Friendly Choices
1. **Best for cutting**: Grilled meats (no bread), baked ta'ameya
2. **Best post-workout**: Koshari (high carbs)
3. **Best protein**: Kebab, Shish Tawook, Kofta
4. **Avoid when cutting**: Fiteer, fried items

## Healthier Ordering Tips
- Ask for grilled (meshwi) not fried (ma'li)
- Request less oil in ful
- Skip fried onions on koshari (-100 cal)
- Choose bran bread over fino
