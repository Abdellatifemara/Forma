## By Fitness Goal

| Goal | Best Choices | Avoid |
|------|--------------|-------|
| Fat Loss | Water, unsweetened tea, karkade (no sugar) | Juices, sodas, sahlab |
| Muscle Gain | Milk, protein shakes, water | Sodas |
| Pre-Workout | Black coffee, green tea | Heavy drinks |
| Post-Workout | Water, milk | Carbonated |

## Calorie Traps
- Traditional tea (3+ sugars) = 70+ cal/cup
- Sahlab = 250 cal (like a small meal!)
- Fresh juices often have added sugar
- Ramadan drinks are very high sugar

## Zero/Low Cal Options
- Plain tea or coffee
- Karkade without sugar
- Water with lemon
- Herbal teas
