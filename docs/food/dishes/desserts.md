## Fitness Considerations
- All Egyptian desserts are HIGH in sugar and calories
- Best enjoyed occasionally, not daily
- **If bulking**: Om Ali provides calories + some protein
- **If cutting**: Fruit sorbet or small mahalabeya
- **Ramadan tip**: Limit to 1 small piece after iftar

## Lower-Calorie Alternatives
1. Fresh fruit instead of sweets
2. Small portion mahalabeya (lowest cal pudding)
3. Roz bil laban made with skim milk
4. Fruit sorbet instead of ice cream
5. Date with nuts (natural energy)
