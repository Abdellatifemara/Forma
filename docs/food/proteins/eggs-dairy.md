## Best Choices by Goal

| Goal | Best Choices |
|------|--------------|
| Fat Loss | Egg whites, Areesh, Greek 0%, skim milk |
| Muscle Gain | Whole eggs, Greek yogurt, Roumy |
| Low Sodium | Areesh, Labneh |
| Budget | Eggs, Zabadi, White cheese |

## Daily Recommendations
- Eggs: 2-4 whole eggs/day is fine for most people
- Dairy: 2-3 servings/day for calcium
- Greek yogurt: Excellent protein source (use as snack)
