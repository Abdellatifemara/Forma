## Protein Quality
Legumes are incomplete proteins. Combine with:
- Rice (ful + rice = complete protein)
- Bread (ful sandwich)
- Other grains

## Fitness Tips
- **Fat loss**: Ful (no oil) - high protein, high fiber, filling
- **Muscle gain**: Add eggs/meat to legume dishes
- **Post-workout**: Koshari (high carb + moderate protein)
- **Budget protein**: Lentils are cheapest protein source

## Cooking Tips
- Soak dried legumes overnight
- Pressure cooker saves time
- Season with cumin for digestion
- Start small portions if not used to fiber
