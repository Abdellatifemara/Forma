# Balance & Stability Training

## Why Balance Matters
- Injury prevention
- Better lifting form
- Improved proprioception
- Athletic performance
- Daily life function
- Core strength development

## Balance Assessments

### Single Leg Stand Test
**How to:**
1. Stand on one leg
2. Other foot off ground
3. Hands on hips
4. Eyes open, then closed

**Standards:**
| Age | Eyes Open | Eyes Closed |
|-----|-----------|-------------|
| 20-40 | 45 sec+ | 15 sec+ |
| 40-60 | 40 sec+ | 10 sec+ |
| 60+ | 30 sec+ | 5 sec+ |

### Y-Balance Test
- Stand on one leg
- Reach other foot in 3 directions
- Anterior, posterolateral, posteromedial
- Compare sides for asymmetry

## Balance Exercises - Beginner

### Single Leg Stand
**Progression:**
1. Hold support → 30s
2. No support → 30s
3. Eyes closed → 15s
4. Unstable surface → 30s

### Tandem Stance
**How to:**
1. Heel-to-toe position
2. Arms at sides
3. Hold 30 seconds
4. Switch which foot is in front

### Weight Shifts
**How to:**
1. Stand feet hip-width
2. Shift weight side to side
3. Then front to back
4. Control the movement

### Toe/Heel Raises
**How to:**
1. Stand tall
2. Rise onto toes, hold 2s
3. Lower, rock back onto heels
4. 10-15 reps

## Balance Exercises - Intermediate

### Single Leg Romanian Deadlift
**How to:**
1. Stand on one leg
2. Hinge at hips
3. Extend back leg behind
4. Reach toward ground
5. Return to standing
6. 8-12 reps each leg

### Single Leg Squat (Assisted)
**How to:**
1. Stand on one leg
2. Hold support
3. Squat down on one leg
4. Keep knee tracking over toes
5. Stand back up
6. 6-10 reps each leg

### BOSU Ball Stand
**How to:**
1. Stand on BOSU (dome side up)
2. Find balance
3. Progress to single leg
4. Add movements (squats, reaches)

### Single Leg Hop & Hold
**How to:**
1. Hop forward on one leg
2. Stick the landing
3. Hold stable for 3 seconds
4. Hop again
5. 5-8 hops each leg

## Balance Exercises - Advanced

### Single Leg Squat (Pistol)
**How to:**
1. Stand on one leg
2. Extend other leg forward
3. Squat all the way down
4. Stand back up
5. Assistance as needed

### Single Leg Deadlift with Reach
**How to:**
1. Single leg RDL
2. At bottom, reach in different directions
3. Multi-planar challenge
4. 6 reps with reaches each leg

### Reactive Balance
**How to:**
1. Stand on one leg
2. Partner gives small pushes
3. React and maintain balance
4. Progress intensity

### Eyes Closed Challenges
- Single leg stand eyes closed
- BOSU stand eyes closed
- Tandem walk eyes closed

## Stability Training

### Pallof Press (Anti-Rotation)
**How to:**
1. Cable or band at chest height
2. Stand perpendicular to anchor
3. Press arms straight out
4. Resist rotation
5. Hold 5 seconds
6. 8-10 reps each side

### Single Arm Farmer Carry
**How to:**
1. Hold weight in one hand
2. Walk with tall posture
3. Resist leaning to weighted side
4. 30-40 yards each side

### Bird Dog
**How to:**
1. Hands and knees position
2. Extend opposite arm and leg
3. Keep back flat
4. Hold 3-5 seconds
5. Return, switch sides
6. 10 reps each side

### Dead Bug
**How to:**
1. Lie on back
2. Arms up, knees bent 90°
3. Lower opposite arm and leg
4. Keep back pressed to floor
5. Return, alternate
6. 10 reps each side

## Integration with Training

### Warm-Up Addition
- Single leg stands: 30s each
- Bird dog: 10 each side
- Weight shifts: 20 total

### Exercise Modifications
| Standard | Balance Challenge |
|----------|-------------------|
| Squat | Single leg squat |
| Deadlift | Single leg RDL |
| Row | Single arm row |
| Press | Half-kneeling press |
| Curl | Single leg stance curl |

### Programming
- Include 1-2 balance exercises per workout
- Progress difficulty gradually
- Train both sides equally
- Focus on quality over quantity

## Common Balance Mistakes
| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Holding breath | Breathe normally |
| Looking down | Eyes forward, fixed point |
| Rushing | Slow, controlled movements |
| Gripping toes | Relax feet, use whole foot |
| One-sided only | Train both sides equally |
