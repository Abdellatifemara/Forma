## Quick Flows by Time

### 5-Minute Wake-Up
1. Cat-cow (1 min)
2. Downward dog (30s)
3. Sun Sal A (2x)
4. Forward fold (30s)
5. Mountain (30s)

### 10-Minute Desk Break
1. Neck rolls (30s)
2. Seated twist (30s each)
3. Standing forward fold (30s)
4. Low lunge (30s each)
5. Downward dog (45s)
6. Child's pose (60s)
7. Seated meditation (2 min)

### 30-Minute Full Practice
1. Meditation (3 min)
2. Warm-up (3 min)
3. Sun Sal A x5 (5 min)
4. Sun Sal B x3 (5 min)
5. Standing sequence (6 min)
6. Floor/hip openers (5 min)
7. Savasana (3 min)
