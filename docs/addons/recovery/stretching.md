# Static Stretching Guide

## When to Stretch
| Timing | Type | Duration |
|--------|------|----------|
| Pre-workout | Dynamic | 5-10 min |
| Post-workout | Static | 10-15 min |
| Off days | Static/Yoga | 20-30 min |
| Before bed | Gentle static | 10 min |

## Upper Body Stretches

### Chest/Pec Stretch
**Doorway:**
1. Arm on doorframe at 90Â°
2. Step through doorway
3. Feel stretch in chest
4. Hold 30-45 seconds
5. Repeat higher/lower for different fibers

### Lat Stretch
**Standing:**
1. Grab sturdy object overhead
2. Drop hips back and to side
3. Feel stretch along side
4. Hold 30-45 seconds each side

### Tricep Stretch
1. Reach arm overhead
2. Bend elbow, hand behind head
3. Use other hand to push elbow back
4. Hold 30 seconds each arm

### Bicep Stretch
1. Face wall, arm extended
2. Turn body away from arm
3. Feel stretch in bicep/forearm
4. Hold 30 seconds each arm

### Shoulder Stretch (Cross Body)
1. Bring arm across chest
2. Use other hand to pull elbow closer
3. Keep shoulder down
4. Hold 30 seconds each

### Neck Stretches
**Lateral:**
1. Tilt head to side, ear to shoulder
2. Gentle pressure with hand
3. Hold 20-30 seconds each side

**Rotation:**
1. Turn head to look over shoulder
2. Hold 20 seconds each side

## Lower Body Stretches

### Quad Stretch
**Standing:**
1. Hold foot behind you
2. Keep knees together
3. Squeeze glute for deeper stretch
4. Hold 30-45 seconds each

**Lying:**
1. Lie on side
2. Pull top foot to glute
3. Push hip forward
4. Hold 45 seconds each

### Hamstring Stretch
**Seated:**
1. Legs extended
2. Reach toward toes
3. Hinge at hips, not rounding back
4. Hold 45-60 seconds

**Standing:**
1. Foot on raised surface
2. Keep leg straight
3. Hinge forward at hips
4. Hold 30-45 seconds each

### Hip Flexor Stretch
**Kneeling:**
1. Kneel on one knee
2. Front foot forward
3. Squeeze back glute
4. Push hips forward
5. Hold 45-60 seconds each

### Glute Stretch
**Figure 4:**
1. Lie on back
2. Cross ankle over opposite knee
3. Pull bottom leg toward chest
4. Hold 45 seconds each

**Pigeon:**
1. Front shin across body
2. Back leg extended
3. Fold forward over front leg
4. Hold 60-90 seconds each

### Calf Stretch
**Gastrocnemius (straight leg):**
1. Wall push, back leg straight
2. Heel on ground
3. Hold 30 seconds each

**Soleus (bent knee):**
1. Same position
2. Bend back knee slightly
3. Hold 30 seconds each

### Adductor/Groin Stretch
**Butterfly:**
1. Soles of feet together
2. Knees out to sides
3. Press knees down gently
4. Hold 45 seconds

**Side Lunge:**
1. Wide stance
2. Shift weight to one side
3. Keep other leg straight
4. Hold 30 seconds each
