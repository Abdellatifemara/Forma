# Lower Body Mobility

## Hip Mobility

### Assessment Tests
| Test | Pass | Fail Indicates |
|------|------|----------------|
| Deep Squat | Full depth, heels down | Ankle/hip restriction |
| 90/90 Position | Comfortable hold | Hip rotation deficit |
| Thomas Test | Leg flat on table | Tight hip flexors |
| Toe Touch | Touch toes | Hamstring/posterior chain |

### Hip Circles
**How to:**
1. Stand on one leg (hold support)
2. Lift opposite knee to hip height
3. Circle knee outward, then back
4. 10 circles each direction
5. Switch legs

**Purpose:** Hip ROM warm-up

### 90/90 Hip Switch
**How to:**
1. Sit with both legs at 90°
2. Front leg: external rotation
3. Back leg: internal rotation
4. Switch sides without using hands
5. 10 switches

**Purpose:** Hip internal/external rotation

### Hip CARs
**How to:**
1. Stand tall, hold support
2. Lift knee high in front
3. Rotate out to side (like fire hydrant)
4. Circle back behind body
5. Return to start
6. 5 reps each direction, each leg

**Purpose:** Full hip ROM, joint health

### Frog Stretch
**How to:**
1. Hands and knees, knees wide
2. Feet turned out
3. Rock hips back toward heels
4. Keep spine neutral
5. Hold 60-90 seconds

**Purpose:** Hip adductors, inner thigh

### Pigeon Stretch
**How to:**
1. Front shin across body
2. Back leg extended behind
3. Square hips to front
4. Fold forward over front leg
5. Hold 60-90 seconds each side

**Purpose:** Hip external rotation, glutes

## Ankle Mobility

### Assessment
- Knee-to-wall test: 4-5 inches = good

### Ankle Circles
- 10 each direction, each foot
- Point and flex toes

### Knee-to-Wall Stretch
**How to:**
1. Face wall, foot 4 inches away
2. Keep heel down
3. Drive knee toward wall
4. Progress distance as mobility improves
5. Hold 30 seconds or 10-15 reps

**Purpose:** Ankle dorsiflexion

### Calf Stretch (Straight Leg)
**How to:**
1. Hands on wall
2. Step one foot back
3. Keep back leg straight, heel down
4. Lean into wall
5. Hold 30-45 seconds

**Purpose:** Gastrocnemius stretch

### Calf Stretch (Bent Knee)
**How to:**
1. Same as above
2. But bend back knee slightly
3. Keep heel on ground
4. Hold 30-45 seconds

**Purpose:** Soleus stretch

## Hamstring Mobility

### Standing Hamstring Stretch
**How to:**
1. Foot on elevated surface
2. Keep leg straight
3. Hinge at hips, reach toward foot
4. Hold 30-45 seconds

### Lying Hamstring Stretch
**How to:**
1. Lie on back
2. Lift one leg straight up
3. Use strap/towel behind thigh
4. Pull toward chest
5. Keep opposite leg flat
6. Hold 45-60 seconds

### Active Hamstring Stretch
**How to:**
1. Lie on back, one leg up
2. Actively pull leg toward chest
3. Hold 2 seconds, release
4. 10-15 reps each leg

## Quad/Hip Flexor Mobility

### Couch Stretch
**How to:**
1. Kneel facing away from wall/couch
2. Place back foot on wall/couch
3. Other foot forward in lunge
4. Squeeze glute, tuck pelvis
5. Hold 60-90 seconds each side

**Purpose:** Deep hip flexor (psoas) and quad

### Half Kneeling Hip Flexor Stretch
**How to:**
1. Kneel on one knee
2. Front foot forward, 90°
3. Squeeze back glute
4. Shift hips forward
5. Hold 30-45 seconds

### Quad Stretch (Standing)
**How to:**
1. Stand on one leg
2. Pull heel to glute
3. Keep knees together
4. Hold support if needed
5. 30 seconds each
