# Upper Body Mobility

## Shoulder Mobility

### Assessment Tests
| Test | Pass | Fail Indicates |
|------|------|----------------|
| Wall Angels | Arms touch wall throughout | Tight pecs/lats |
| Shoulder Flexion | Arms reach overhead (180°) | Lat/shoulder restriction |
| Behind Back Reach | Hands meet behind back | Tight shoulders |
| Sleeper Stretch Test | 70°+ internal rotation | Tight posterior capsule |

### Shoulder Circles
**How to:**
1. Arms at sides
2. Roll shoulders forward 10x
3. Roll shoulders backward 10x
4. Increase circle size gradually

**Purpose:** Warm-up, synovial fluid distribution

### Wall Slides
**How to:**
1. Back against wall
2. Arms in "goalpost" position
3. Slide arms up overhead
4. Keep contact with wall
5. 10-15 reps

**Purpose:** Scapular mobility, shoulder flexion

### Thread the Needle
**How to:**
1. Start on all fours
2. Reach right arm under body to left
3. Lower right shoulder to ground
4. Hold 30 seconds
5. Switch sides

**Purpose:** Thoracic rotation

### Shoulder CARs (Controlled Articular Rotations)
**How to:**
1. Stand tall, arm at side
2. Raise arm forward to overhead
3. Rotate to side (like backstroke)
4. Lower behind body
5. Return to start
6. 5 reps each direction

**Purpose:** Full shoulder ROM, joint health

## Thoracic Spine Mobility

### Cat-Cow
**How to:**
1. Hands and knees position
2. Inhale: arch back, look up (cow)
3. Exhale: round back, tuck chin (cat)
4. 10-15 reps

**Purpose:** Spinal flexion/extension

### Thoracic Extensions on Foam Roller
**How to:**
1. Foam roller under upper back
2. Hands behind head
3. Extend back over roller
4. Don't hyperextend lower back
5. Move roller up/down spine
6. 10 reps at each position

**Purpose:** T-spine extension

### Open Book
**How to:**
1. Lie on side, knees bent 90°
2. Arms stacked in front
3. Rotate top arm open to other side
4. Follow with eyes
5. Return to start
6. 10 reps each side

**Purpose:** Thoracic rotation

### Quadruped T-Spine Rotation
**How to:**
1. All fours, one hand behind head
2. Rotate elbow toward opposite knee
3. Then rotate open toward ceiling
4. Follow elbow with eyes
5. 10 reps each side

**Purpose:** Active thoracic rotation

## Wrist Mobility

### Wrist Circles
- 10 circles each direction
- Make fists, rotate at wrist

### Wrist Flexor Stretch
1. Arm straight in front
2. Pull fingers back with other hand
3. Hold 30 seconds

### Wrist Extensor Stretch
1. Arm straight in front
2. Bend wrist down, pull with other hand
3. Hold 30 seconds

### Prayer/Reverse Prayer
1. Palms together in front of chest
2. Lower hands, keeping palms together
3. Then reverse (backs of hands together)
4. Hold 15-20 seconds each
