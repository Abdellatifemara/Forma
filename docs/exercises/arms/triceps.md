# Triceps Exercises (85+ Movements)

## Anatomy

### Triceps Brachii
The three-headed muscle on the back of the upper arm. **Triceps make up 67% of arm size!**

**Three Heads:**

**Long Head** (Largest)
- Origin: Infraglenoid tubercle of scapula
- Crosses both shoulder and elbow joints
- Activated most with arms overhead
- Creates the "hanging" mass of the arm

**Lateral Head** (Outer)
- Origin: Posterior humerus (above radial groove)
- Creates the "horseshoe" shape
- Most visible from the side

**Medial Head** (Deep)
- Origin: Posterior humerus (below radial groove)
- Stabilizer, works in all tricep movements
- Least visible but important for strength

**Insertion:** All three heads insert on the olecranon process of ulna

**Function:** Elbow extension (straightening the arm)

## PRESSING MOVEMENTS (14)

### TR001 - Close-Grip Bench Press
| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Equipment** | Barbell + Flat Bench |
| **Difficulty** | Intermediate |
| **Primary** | All triceps heads |
| **Secondary** | Chest, anterior delt |
| **Contraindications** | Wrist pain, shoulder issues |

**Form Cues:**
- Grip shoulder-width or slightly narrower
- Keep elbows tucked at 30-45° from body
- Lower bar to lower chest
- Press up while focusing on triceps
- Full lockout at top

**Common Mistakes:**
- Grip too narrow → Wrist strain, use shoulder width
- Elbows flaring → Keep tucked
- Not locking out → Full extension for triceps

## Tricep Head Targeting Guide

### Long Head (Largest - prioritize this!)
Best exercises:
- Overhead extensions (TR027-TR040)
- Skull crushers behind head (TR023)
- Cable overhead work (TR032-TR034)

**Key:** Arms must be overhead for full stretch

### Lateral Head (Horseshoe)
Best exercises:
- Pushdowns (TR041-TR056)
- Dips with upright torso (TR065)
- Kickbacks (TR057-TR064)

### Medial Head
Best exercises:
- Reverse grip pushdowns (TR045)
- Close-grip pressing (TR001-TR014)
- Works in all tricep movements

## Key Training Principles

1. **Prioritize long head**: Include overhead work every session
2. **Full lockout**: Triceps work hardest at full extension
3. **Elbow health**: Control the weight, avoid ego lifting
4. **Volume matters**: Triceps can handle 12-20 sets/week
5. **Compound first**: Start with pressing, finish with isolation

## Common Mistakes
| Mistake | Problem | Fix |
|---------|---------|-----|
| Skipping overhead work | Long head underdeveloped | Include extensions |
| Not locking out | Missing peak contraction | Full extension |
| Too heavy on isolation | Elbow strain | Lighter, more control |
| Elbows moving | Using shoulders | Pin elbows in place |
| Only pushdowns | Incomplete development | Vary angles |

## Programming Recommendations
| Goal | Sets/Week | Rep Range | Rest |
|------|-----------|-----------|------|
| Strength | 8-12 | 4-8 | 2-3 min |
| Hypertrophy | 12-18 | 8-15 | 60-90s |
| Endurance | 15-20 | 15-25 | 30-45s |

**Optimal Weekly Volume:** 10-18 direct sets (plus indirect from pressing)
