# Complete Warmup Exercises Database

## Overview

A proper warmup prepares the body for exercise by increasing blood flow, raising core temperature, activating the nervous system, and preparing joints and muscles for movement.

### Benefits of Warming Up
- Increased blood flow to muscles
- Elevated core temperature
- Enhanced nerve conduction
- Improved joint lubrication
- Better muscle elasticity
- Reduced injury risk
- Mental preparation

### Warmup Structure
1. **General cardio** (2-3 min) - Raise heart rate
2. **Dynamic stretches** (3-5 min) - Improve mobility
3. **Activation drills** (3-5 min) - Wake up key muscles
4. **Movement prep** (2-3 min) - Sport/workout specific

## General Cardio Warmup

### WU001 - Jogging in Place
- **Duration**: 30-60 seconds
- **Primary**: Full body cardio
- **Form Cues**:
  - Light jog, stay on balls of feet
  - Arms swing naturally
  - Land softly
  - Gradually increase pace
- **Purpose**: Raise heart rate, increase blood flow

### WU002 - Jumping Jacks
- **Reps**: 20-30
- **Primary**: Full body cardio
- **Form Cues**:
  - Start with feet together, arms at sides
  - Jump feet wide, arms overhead
  - Jump back to start
  - Land softly, continuous motion
- **Purpose**: Elevate heart rate, full body activation

### WU003 - High Knees
- **Duration**: 30-45 seconds
- **Primary**: Hip flexors, cardio
- **Form Cues**:
  - Drive knees up toward chest
  - Quick alternating motion
  - Opposite arm drives with each knee
  - Stay on balls of feet
- **Purpose**: Hip activation, elevate heart rate

### WU004 - Butt Kicks
- **Duration**: 30-45 seconds
- **Primary**: Hamstrings, quads
- **Form Cues**:
  - Kick heels toward glutes
  - Quick alternating motion
  - Stay upright, don't lean forward
  - Arms swing naturally
- **Purpose**: Hamstring activation, quad stretch

### WU005 - Seal Jacks
- **Reps**: 15-20
- **Primary**: Shoulders, chest
- **Form Cues**:
  - Like jumping jacks but arms go horizontal
  - Bring arms together in front
  - Opens and closes chest
- **Purpose**: Shoulder and chest warmup

### WU006 - Skipping
- **Distance**: 20-30 meters
- **Primary**: Coordination, calves
- **Form Cues**:
  - Exaggerated skipping motion
  - Drive knee up with each skip
  - Swing opposite arm
  - Cover ground forward
- **Purpose**: Coordination, ankle warmup

### WU007 - Lateral Shuffle
- **Distance**: 10 meters each way
- **Primary**: Adductors, abductors
- **Form Cues**:
  - Athletic stance, shuffled sideways
  - Push off trailing leg
  - Stay low, feet don't cross
  - Quick, controlled movement
- **Purpose**: Lateral movement prep, hip activation

### WU008 - Carioca (Grapevine)
- **Distance**: 10 meters each way
- **Primary**: Hip rotation, coordination
- **Form Cues**:
  - Move laterally, crossing feet
  - Alternate front cross and back cross
  - Rotate hips to allow crossing
  - Arms assist with rotation
- **Purpose**: Hip rotation warmup

### WU009 - Backpedal
- **Distance**: 15-20 meters
- **Primary**: Hamstrings, coordination
- **Form Cues**:
  - Run backward with control
  - Stay on balls of feet
  - Keep chest up, glance back occasionally
  - Arms move naturally
- **Purpose**: Hamstring activation, spatial awareness

### WU010 - March in Place
- **Duration**: 30-45 seconds
- **Primary**: Hip flexors
- **Form Cues**:
  - Controlled march, not jogging
  - Lift knees to hip height
  - Opposite arm swings
  - Stay tall
- **Purpose**: Gentle hip flexor activation

## Dynamic Stretches

### WU011 - Arm Circles (Small to Large)
- **Reps**: 10 each direction
- **Primary**: Shoulders
- **Form Cues**:
  - Start with small circles
  - Progressively make larger
  - Forward then backward
  - Keep core engaged
- **Purpose**: Shoulder joint warmup

### WU012 - Arm Swings (Horizontal)
- **Reps**: 15-20
- **Primary**: Chest, shoulders
- **Form Cues**:
  - Swing arms across body then open wide
  - Alternating which arm is on top
  - Dynamic, controlled movement
- **Purpose**: Chest and shoulder mobility

### WU013 - Leg Swings (Front/Back)
- **Reps**: 10-15 per leg
- **Primary**: Hip flexors, hamstrings
- **Form Cues**:
  - Hold wall/support for balance
  - Swing leg forward and back like pendulum
  - Keep leg relatively straight
  - Gradually increase range
- **Purpose**: Hip mobility, hamstring warmup

### WU014 - Leg Swings (Side to Side)
- **Reps**: 10-15 per leg
- **Primary**: Adductors, abductors
- **Form Cues**:
  - Face wall, swing leg side to side
  - Cross in front of body each swing
  - Keep hips relatively stable
  - Controlled movement
- **Purpose**: Hip mobility, groin warmup

### WU015 - Walking Knee Hug
- **Reps**: 10 per leg
- **Primary**: Glutes, hip flexors
- **Form Cues**:
  - Walk forward, pull each knee to chest
  - Hug shin, rise onto toes of standing leg
  - Release and step, repeat other side
- **Purpose**: Glute activation, hip flexor stretch

### WU016 - Walking Quad Pull
- **Reps**: 10 per leg
- **Primary**: Quads, hip flexors
- **Form Cues**:
  - Walk forward, pull heel to glute
  - Rise onto toes of standing leg
  - Extend hip for deeper stretch
  - Release and step forward
- **Purpose**: Quad stretch, balance

### WU017 - Walking Lunge with Twist
- **Reps**: 10 per side
- **Primary**: Hip flexors, thoracic spine
- **Form Cues**:
  - Lunge forward
  - Rotate torso toward front leg
  - Arms can extend for more rotation
  - Stand and step to next lunge
- **Purpose**: Hip mobility, thoracic rotation

### WU018 - Inchworm
- **Reps**: 5-8
- **Primary**: Hamstrings, shoulders
- **Form Cues**:
  - Forward fold, hands to floor
  - Walk hands out to plank
  - Walk feet to hands
  - Stand and repeat
- **Purpose**: Hamstring and shoulder warmup

### WU019 - World's Greatest Stretch
- **Reps**: 5 per side
- **Primary**: Full body mobility
- **Form Cues**:
  - Lunge, hands inside front foot
  - Drop back knee, push hip forward
  - Rotate, reach arm to ceiling
  - Return hand, straighten front leg (hamstring)
  - Return to start
- **Purpose**: Complete lower body warmup

### WU020 - Scorpion
- **Reps**: 8-10 per side
- **Primary**: Hip flexors, thoracic rotation
- **Form Cues**:
  - Lie face down, arms out
  - Lift one leg, swing across body
  - Try to touch floor on opposite side
  - Return and switch
- **Purpose**: Hip and spine mobility

### WU021 - Iron Cross
- **Reps**: 8-10 per side
- **Primary**: Adductors, lower back
- **Form Cues**:
  - Lie on back, arms out
  - Lift one leg, swing across body
  - Keep both shoulders down
  - Return and switch
- **Purpose**: Adductor and lower back mobility

### WU022 - Hip Circles (Standing)
- **Reps**: 10 each direction per hip
- **Primary**: Hip mobility
- **Form Cues**:
  - Stand on one leg, use wall if needed
  - Circle lifted knee in large circles
  - Forward and backward
  - Keep pelvis stable
- **Purpose**: Hip joint warmup

### WU023 - Ankle Circles
- **Reps**: 10 each direction per ankle
- **Primary**: Ankle mobility
- **Form Cues**:
  - Lift one foot off floor
  - Circle ankle in full range
  - Both directions
- **Purpose**: Ankle joint preparation

### WU024 - Neck Circles (Gentle)
- **Reps**: 5 each direction
- **Primary**: Cervical spine
- **Form Cues**:
  - Slow, controlled circles
  - Avoid extreme extension
  - Smooth continuous motion
- **Purpose**: Neck mobility

### WU025 - Trunk Rotations
- **Reps**: 10-15 each direction
- **Primary**: Thoracic spine
- **Form Cues**:
  - Stand with arms crossed on chest
  - Rotate torso side to side
  - Keep hips facing forward
  - Controlled speed
- **Purpose**: Thoracic rotation warmup

## Activation Drills

### WU026 - Glute Bridge Hold
- **Duration**: 20-30 seconds
- **Primary**: Glutes
- **Form Cues**:
  - Lie on back, feet flat
  - Push through heels, lift hips
  - Squeeze glutes hard at top
  - Hold position
- **Purpose**: Glute activation before lower body work

### WU027 - Glute Bridge March
- **Reps**: 10 per leg
- **Primary**: Glutes, core
- **Form Cues**:
  - Hold bridge position
  - March by lifting one foot at a time
  - Keep hips level
  - Squeeze glute of down leg
- **Purpose**: Single-leg glute activation

### WU028 - Clamshell
- **Reps**: 15-20 per side
- **Primary**: Gluteus medius
- **Form Cues**:
  - Side-lying, knees bent 90 degrees
  - Feet together, open top knee
  - Keep hips stacked, don't roll back
  - Controlled movement
- **Purpose**: Hip abductor activation

### WU029 - Fire Hydrant
- **Reps**: 10-15 per side
- **Primary**: Gluteus medius
- **Form Cues**:
  - All fours position
  - Lift knee out to side, keeping bent
  - Keep hips square to floor
  - Return with control
- **Purpose**: Hip abductor activation

### WU030 - Donkey Kick
- **Reps**: 10-15 per side
- **Primary**: Glutes
- **Form Cues**:
  - All fours position
  - Drive heel toward ceiling
  - Keep knee bent at 90 degrees
  - Squeeze glute at top
- **Purpose**: Glute max activation

### WU031 - Bird Dog
- **Reps**: 8-10 per side
- **Primary**: Core, multifidus
- **Form Cues**:
  - All fours, neutral spine
  - Extend opposite arm and leg
  - Keep hips level
  - Hold briefly at top
- **Purpose**: Core and back activation

### WU032 - Dead Bug
- **Reps**: 8-10 per side
- **Primary**: Core
- **Form Cues**:
  - Lie on back, arms up, knees over hips
  - Lower opposite arm and leg toward floor
  - Keep lower back pressed into floor
  - Return and alternate
- **Purpose**: Deep core activation

### WU033 - Plank Hold
- **Duration**: 20-30 seconds
- **Primary**: Core
- **Form Cues**:
  - Forearms or hands on floor
  - Body in straight line
  - Core engaged, glutes squeezed
  - Don't let hips sag
- **Purpose**: Full core activation

### WU034 - Side Plank Hold
- **Duration**: 15-20 seconds per side
- **Primary**: Obliques
- **Form Cues**:
  - Stack feet or stagger
  - Lift hips, create straight line
  - Don't let hips drop
- **Purpose**: Lateral core activation

### WU035 - Scapular Push-Ups
- **Reps**: 10-15
- **Primary**: Serratus anterior
- **Form Cues**:
  - Push-up position, arms straight
  - Only move shoulder blades
  - Protract (push away) then retract (pinch)
  - Don't bend elbows
- **Purpose**: Scapular activation for pressing

### WU036 - Band Pull-Apart
- **Reps**: 15-20
- **Primary**: Rear delts, rhomboids
- **Form Cues**:
  - Hold band at shoulder height
  - Pull apart, bringing band to chest
  - Squeeze shoulder blades
  - Control return
- **Purpose**: Upper back activation

### WU037 - Face Pull (Band)
- **Reps**: 15-20
- **Primary**: Rear delts, rotator cuff
- **Form Cues**:
  - Band attached at face height
  - Pull toward face, elbows high
  - External rotate at end
  - Control return
- **Purpose**: Shoulder and rotator cuff activation

### WU038 - Wall Slide
- **Reps**: 10-15
- **Primary**: Scapular muscles
- **Form Cues**:
  - Back against wall
  - Arms in W position, pressed to wall
  - Slide up to Y, keeping contact
  - Controlled descent
- **Purpose**: Shoulder mobility and activation

### WU039 - Shoulder Pass-Through
- **Reps**: 10-15
- **Primary**: Shoulders, chest
- **Form Cues**:
  - Hold stick or band wide
  - Pass over and behind head
  - Keep arms straight
  - Narrow grip = more challenging
- **Purpose**: Shoulder mobility

### WU040 - Cat-Cow
- **Reps**: 10-12 cycles
- **Primary**: Spine
- **Form Cues**:
  - All fours position
  - Round spine (cat), then arch (cow)
  - Move slowly, feel each segment
- **Purpose**: Spinal mobility

## Movement Prep (Sport Specific)

### Pre-Squat Warm-Up

### WU041 - Goblet Squat Hold
- **Duration**: 30-60 seconds
- **Primary**: Squat pattern
- **Form Cues**:
  - Light weight or bodyweight
  - Sit in bottom of squat
  - Use elbows to push knees out
  - Move around in bottom position
- **Purpose**: Squat groove warmup

### WU042 - Wall Ankle Mobilization
- **Reps**: 15-20 per ankle
- **Primary**: Ankle dorsiflexion
- **Form Cues**:
  - Foot few inches from wall
  - Drive knee forward over toes
  - Keep heel down
  - Touch wall if possible
- **Purpose**: Ankle mobility for squatting

### WU043 - Bodyweight Squats
- **Reps**: 15-20
- **Primary**: Squat pattern
- **Form Cues**:
  - Full depth squats
  - Arms can extend forward for balance
  - Focus on form, not speed
- **Purpose**: Pattern warmup

### Pre-Deadlift Warm-Up

### WU044 - Hip Hinge with Dowel
- **Reps**: 10-15
- **Primary**: Hip hinge pattern
- **Form Cues**:
  - Dowel along spine (head, back, sacrum)
  - Hinge at hips, maintaining contact
  - Slight knee bend
  - Return to standing
- **Purpose**: Hinge pattern warmup

### WU045 - Good Morning (Bodyweight)
- **Reps**: 10-15
- **Primary**: Hamstrings, hinge pattern
- **Form Cues**:
  - Hands behind head or crossed on chest
  - Hinge forward, feeling hamstring stretch
  - Return to standing
- **Purpose**: Hamstring and hip warmup

### WU046 - Pull-Through (Band)
- **Reps**: 15-20
- **Primary**: Glutes, hinge pattern
- **Form Cues**:
  - Band between legs, anchored behind
  - Hinge back, then drive hips forward
  - Squeeze glutes at top
- **Purpose**: Hip hinge with glute focus

### Pre-Bench/Push Warm-Up

### WU047 - Push-Up Plus
- **Reps**: 10-15
- **Primary**: Serratus, chest
- **Form Cues**:
  - Standard push-up
  - At top, push even further (protract scapulae)
  - Feel shoulder blades spread apart
- **Purpose**: Serratus activation for pressing

### WU048 - Band Chest Fly
- **Reps**: 15-20
- **Primary**: Chest
- **Form Cues**:
  - Band around back, hold ends
  - Press forward and together
  - Control return
- **Purpose**: Chest activation

### WU049 - I-Y-T-W Raises (Prone)
- **Reps**: 5-8 each position
- **Primary**: Rotator cuff, upper back
- **Form Cues**:
  - Lie face down
  - I: Arms straight overhead, lift
  - Y: Arms at 45 degrees, lift
  - T: Arms straight out, lift
  - W: Elbows bent, external rotate
- **Purpose**: Shoulder stability activation

### Pre-Running Warm-Up

### WU050 - A-Skip
- **Distance**: 20 meters
- **Primary**: Hip flexors, coordination
- **Form Cues**:
  - Exaggerated skipping
  - Drive knee up high
  - Quick ground contact
- **Purpose**: Running mechanics warmup

### WU051 - B-Skip
- **Distance**: 20 meters
- **Primary**: Hamstrings, hip flexors
- **Form Cues**:
  - A-skip with leg extension at top
  - Cycle leg down actively
  - Pawing motion
- **Purpose**: Running mechanics warmup

### WU052 - Straight Leg Bound
- **Distance**: 20 meters
- **Primary**: Hamstrings, calves
- **Form Cues**:
  - Straight leg running/bouncing
  - Quick contacts, stiff legs
  - Active ankle
- **Purpose**: Ankle stiffness for running

### WU053 - Build-Up Sprint
- **Reps**: 2-3 x 40-60 meters
- **Primary**: Full running pattern
- **Form Cues**:
  - Start slow, accelerate to 80% max
  - Focus on form, not full speed
  - Decelerate gradually
- **Purpose**: Full running prep

## Sample Warmup Routines

### General Full Body Warmup (10 min)
1. Jumping jacks (30 sec)
2. High knees (30 sec)
3. Arm circles (20 each)
4. Leg swings front/back (10 each)
5. Leg swings side to side (10 each)
6. Walking lunge with twist (10 total)
7. Inchworm (5 reps)
8. Glute bridges (10 reps)
9. Dead bugs (10 total)
10. Plank hold (30 sec)

### Pre-Squat/Lower Body (8 min)
1. March in place (30 sec)
2. Hip circles (10 each direction)
3. Leg swings (10 each direction per leg)
4. Goblet squat hold (60 sec)
5. Wall ankle mobilization (15 per ankle)
6. Glute bridges (15 reps)
7. Bodyweight squats (15 reps)
8. Walking lunges (10 total)

### Pre-Deadlift (8 min)
1. Light cardio (60 sec)
2. Hip hinge with dowel (10 reps)
3. Good mornings (10 reps)
4. Leg swings (10 each)
5. Glute bridges (15 reps)
6. Dead bugs (10 total)
7. Band pull-throughs (15 reps)
8. Bodyweight RDLs (10 reps)

### Pre-Upper Body Push (8 min)
1. Arm circles (15 each direction)
2. Arm swings (20 reps)
3. Shoulder pass-throughs (10 reps)
4. Band pull-aparts (15 reps)
5. Scapular push-ups (10 reps)
6. Push-up plus (10 reps)
7. Band face pulls (15 reps)
8. Wall slides (10 reps)

### Pre-Running (10 min)
1. Light jog (2 min)
2. High knees (30 sec)
3. Butt kicks (30 sec)
4. Leg swings (10 each direction)
5. Walking lunges (10 total)
6. A-skips (2 x 20m)
7. B-skips (2 x 20m)
8. Build-up sprints (2-3 x 40m at 70-80%)

**Strength Training**: WU026-40 + specific prep
**Running/Cardio**: WU001-10, WU050-53
**Flexibility/Yoga**: WU011-25, WU040
