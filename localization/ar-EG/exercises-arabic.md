# Egyptian Arabic Localization - Exercises (تمارين)

## Localization Notes

**Dialect:** Egyptian Arabic (العامية المصرية)
**NOT:** Modern Standard Arabic (الفصحى)

**Principle:** Use what Egyptian gym-goers actually say, mixing Arabic with common English fitness terms that are already adopted in Egyptian gym culture.

**For:** FitApp Egypt
